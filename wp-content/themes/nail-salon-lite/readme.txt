== Description ==

Nail Salon WordPress theme for beauty salons, beauty shops, beauty spas, cosmetics, hairdressers, barber, hair stylist, health, lifestyle, massage, salon, spa booking, wellness, and cosmetic stores. Nail spas, nail bars, makeup bars, and nail polish templates, nail care, eyebrow tattooing, eyelash, brow bar, manicure, pedicure, waxing, threading, massage, steam bath, hair care and other saloon related services, skincare products, hair and body cosmetics, fragrances, and health and beauty items. Easy to use via Elementor. Simple, flexible and scalable.
